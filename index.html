Welcome to Golden Dragon

Experience authentic Chinese cuisine prepared by master chefs using traditional recipes passed down through generations. Located in the heart of Chinatown, we offer a warm and inviting atmosphere for family gatherings, business lunches, and romantic dinners.

Our menu features regional specialties from Szechuan, Cantonese, and Beijing culinary traditions. Each dish is prepared fresh daily using the finest ingredients.

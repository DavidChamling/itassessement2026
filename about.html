About Golden Dragon

Golden Dragon was founded in 1985 by Master Chef Li Wei, who brought authentic recipes from his hometown in Beijing. For nearly four decades, we have been serving the community with traditional Chinese cuisine made from the freshest ingredients.

Our Philosophy

We believe that great food starts with quality ingredients. Our chefs prepare each dish from scratch daily, using techniques passed down through generations. The wok hei (breath of the wok) technique gives our stir-fried dishes their distinctive smoky flavor.

Our Team

Executive Chef Ming Zhang leads our kitchen. He is a third-generation chef trained in Beijing. Our front-of-house team ensures every guest feels welcome and well taken care of.
